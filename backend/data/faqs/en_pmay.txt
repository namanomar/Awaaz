Q: Who is eligible for the PM Awas Yojana scheme?
A: To be eligible, your family must not own a pucca house anywhere in India. The scheme is divided into urban and rural components, with eligibility based on income categories for urban areas and selection from specific surveys for rural areas. For urban areas, income categories range from Economically Weaker Section (EWS) to Middle Income Group (MIG-II).

Q: How do I apply for PM Awas Yojana if I live in a city?
A: If you live in an urban area, you can apply for PM Awas Yojana Urban online through the website pmaymis.gov.in. Alternatively, you can visit a Common Service Centre to complete your application.

Q: How do I apply for PM Awas Yojana if I live in a village?
A: For those in rural areas, you can apply for PM Awas Yojana Gramin by visiting your local gram panchayat. You can also submit your application at the block development office.
