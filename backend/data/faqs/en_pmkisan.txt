Q: What is PM Kisan Samman Nidhi?
A: PM Kisan Samman Nidhi is a central government scheme that provides Rs 6,000 per year in direct income support to small and marginal farmer families. The amount is paid in three equal instalments of Rs 2,000 every four months directly to the farmer's bank account. Helpline: 155261 or 011-23381092.

Q: Who is eligible for PM Kisan?
A: All small and marginal farmer families who own cultivable land are eligible. There is no minimum land requirement. The scheme covers landholding farmers with cultivable land as per land records. Institutional landholders, government employees, and income tax payers are not eligible.

Q: How do I apply for PM Kisan?
A: Visit your nearest Common Service Centre or the PM Kisan portal at pmkisan.gov.in. You can also approach your agriculture department office. Self-registration is available online at pmkisan.gov.in/RegistrationForm.aspx.

Q: What documents are needed for PM Kisan registration?
A: You need your Aadhaar card, land ownership records (khata/khasra number), bank account details with IFSC code, and mobile number linked to Aadhaar. Make sure your Aadhaar is linked to your bank account for direct transfer.

Q: How do I check my PM Kisan payment status?
A: Visit pmkisan.gov.in and click on "Beneficiary Status". Enter your Aadhaar number or mobile number or account number to check your payment status and instalment details.

Q: My PM Kisan instalment is not received. What should I do?
A: First check your status at pmkisan.gov.in. Common reasons for non-payment include Aadhaar not linked to bank account, incorrect bank details, or your name not matching Aadhaar. Contact your local agriculture officer or call helpline 155261 to resolve.

Q: Can a farmer from any state apply for PM Kisan?
A: Yes, PM Kisan is a central government scheme available to eligible farmers in all states and union territories of India. Some state governments also have their own supplementary farmer support schemes.
