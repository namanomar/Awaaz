Q: What is PM Ujjwala Yojana?
A: Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) is a scheme to provide free LPG connections to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households. The scheme aims to reduce health hazards caused by cooking with traditional fuels like firewood and dung cakes. Apply at your nearest LPG gas agency.

Q: Who is eligible for PM Ujjwala Yojana?
A: Women aged 18 and above who belong to BPL households are eligible. The household should not already have an LPG connection. Eligibility is based on the SECC 2011 database, PMAY beneficiaries, SC/ST households, or other identified BPL categories.

Q: How do I apply for PM Ujjwala Yojana?
A: Visit your nearest LPG gas agency (IndianOil, HPCL, or BPCL) and ask for the Ujjwala Yojana form (KYC form). Fill and submit the form along with required documents. You can also apply online at pmuy.gov.in.

Q: What documents are needed for Ujjwala Yojana?
A: You need BPL ration card, Aadhaar card, bank account details, and a passport-size photograph. A declaration that no LPG connection exists in the household is also required.

Q: Is the LPG cylinder completely free under Ujjwala Yojana?
A: The connection (deposit, stove, pressure regulator, and first refill) is provided free of cost. However, subsequent LPG cylinder refills are at market price, though you will receive the LPG subsidy in your bank account through the PAHAL scheme.

Q: I belong to a poor family but I am not in the BPL list. Can I still apply?
A: Under Ujjwala 2.0, the scheme has been expanded. If you belong to SC/ST, PM Awas Yojana beneficiaries, Antyodaya Anna Yojana, forest dwellers, or are a migrant family, you may be eligible even without a BPL card. Visit your nearest gas agency to check.

Q: What is the helpline number for Ujjwala Yojana?
A: You can call the LPG helpline at 1906 or the toll-free number 18002333555. For online grievances, visit pgportal.gov.in.
