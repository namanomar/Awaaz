Q: MGNREGA kya hai?
A: MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi Rashtriya Gramin Rozgar Guarantee Adhiniyam) ek sarkari yojana hai jo gramin parivaron ko saal mein 100 din ka rozgar deti hai. Is yojana mein majdoori seedha aapke bank khate mein aati hai. Helpline number 1800-111-555 par call karein.

Q: MGNREGA ke liye kaun eligible hai?
A: Gaon mein rehne wala koi bhi vayask (bada) vyakti jo sharirik kaam karne ka icchhuk ho, MGNREGA ke liye apply kar sakta hai. Aay ki koi seema nahin hai. Gram panchayat mein apna naam register karwayein.

Q: MGNREGA job card kaise banwayen?
A: Apni gram panchayat mein jayen aur ek application form bhar kar jama karein. Form mein ghar ke sabhi bade sadasyon ke naam likhein. 15 din ke andar job card milna chahiye. Job card bilkul muft hai.

Q: MGNREGA mein kaam ki maang kaise karen?
A: Gram panchayat mein likhi hui darkhwast dijiye ki aapko kaam chahiye. Panchayat ko 15 din ke andar kaam dena hoga. Agar 15 din mein kaam na mile toh aapko berojgari bhatta milega.

Q: MGNREGA mein mazdoori ka paisa kab milta hai?
A: Kaam karne ke 15 din ke andar seedha aapke bank khate mein paisa aata hai. Apna bank khata job card se link karwayein. Agar paisa na aaye toh gram panchayat se ya 1800-111-555 helpline par shikayat karein.

Q: MGNREGA ke liye kaunse documents chahiye?
A: Aadhaar card, niwas praman (ration card ya voter ID), aur ek passport size photo chahiye. Parivar ke sabhi bade sadasyon ki jankari deni hogi.

Q: MGNREGA mein ek saal mein kitne din kaam milta hai?
A: Ek parivar ko ek saal mein kum se kum 100 din ka kaam dene ki guarantee hai. Kuch rajyon mein state scheme se zyada din kaam milta hai. 100 din poore hone ke baad agar aur kaam chahiye toh state government se milta hai lekin keendriya guarantee sirf 100 din ki hai.
