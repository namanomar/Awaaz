Q: Budhapa pension (NSAP) ke liye kaise apply karen?
A: NSAP (National Social Assistance Programme) mein budhapa pension, vidhwa pension, aur viklang pension shaamil hai. Iske liye apni gram panchayat ya block development office ya jile ke samaj kalyan vibhag mein jayen aur application form bhar kar jama karen.

Q: Budhapa pension ke liye kaun eligible hai?
A: BPL parivaar ke 60 saal ya usse adhik aayu ke purush aur mahilayein eligible hain. 60-79 saal ke logon ko kendra sarkar ki taraf se Rs 200 pratimaah milte hain. 80 saal se adhik aayu ke logon ko Rs 500 pratimaah milte hain. Rajya sarkar iske upar aur rashi jod sakti hai.

Q: Vidhwa pension ke liye kaun eligible hai?
A: 40-79 saal ki umra ki BPL parivaar ki vidhwa mahilayein Indira Gandhi Rashtriya Vidhwa Pension Yojana ke liye eligible hain. Kendra sarkar ki taraf se Rs 300 pratimaah milte hain.

Q: Viklang pension ke liye kaun eligible hai?
A: BPL parivaar ke 18-79 saal ke vyakti jo 80 praatishat ya adhik vikalang hain, unhe Rs 300 pratimaah ki pension milti hai.

Q: NSAP pension ke liye kaunse documents chahiye?
A: Aadhaar card, aayu praman patra (janm praman patra ya school certificate), BPL/aamdani praman patra, bank khate ki details, aur passport size photo chahiye. Vidhwa pension ke liye pati ka mrityu praman patra. Viklang pension ke liye sarkari aspatal ka vikalangata praman patra.

Q: Pension shuru hone mein kitna samay lagta hai?
A: Application jama karne ke baad aam taur par 60-90 din mein pension shuru ho jaati hai. Pension seedha aapke bank khate mein aati hai. Agar zyada samay lage toh gram panchayat ya samaj kalyan vibhag mein follow up karein.
