Q: PM Kisan Samman Nidhi ke liye kaun eligible hai?
A: Woh sab chhote aur seemaant kisan jo 2 hectare tak zameen rakhte hain PM Kisan ke liye eligible hain. Sarkaari naukar aur income tax bharane wale eligible nahi hain.

Q: PM Kisan ki kishtein kab aati hain?
A: PM Kisan yojana mein saal mein 3 kisht aati hain — har 4 mahine mein 2000 rupaye seedha bank account mein.

Q: PM Kisan registration kaise karein?
A: Apne nazdiki Common Service Centre ya pmkisan.gov.in par online registration karein. Aadhaar, bank passbook aur zameen ke kaagaz zaruri hain.

Q: PM Kisan helpline number kya hai?
A: PM Kisan helpline number 155261 ya 1800-115-526 hai. Yeh toll free number hai.

Q: PM Kisan status kaise check karein?
A: pmkisan.gov.in par Beneficiary Status section mein apna Aadhaar number ya mobile number daalkar status check karein.

Q: PM Kisan mein kitne paise milte hain?
A: PM Kisan Samman Nidhi mein har saal 6000 rupaye milte hain — teen kisht mein, har 4 mahine mein 2000 rupaye seedha aapke bank khate mein aate hain. Paisa check karne ke liye pmkisan.gov.in par Beneficiary Status mein jayen ya helpline 155261 par call karein.

Q: PM Kisan ka paisa kyun nahi aaya?
A: PM Kisan ki rashi na aane ke kaaran mein Aadhaar bank se link na hona, galat bank details, ya naam mismatch ho sakta hai. pmkisan.gov.in par status check karein aur koi problem ho toh helpline 155261 par call karein ya nazdiki agriculture office mein jayen.
