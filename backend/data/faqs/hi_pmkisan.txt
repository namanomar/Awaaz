Q: PM Kisan Samman Nidhi kya hai?
A: PM Kisan Samman Nidhi ek sarkari yojana hai jo chhote aur seemaant kisaan parivaar ko saal mein Rs 6,000 ki direct income support deti hai. Paisa teen barabar hisson mein Rs 2,000 har chaar mahine mein kisaan ke bank account mein direct jama kiya jata hai. Helpline: 155261 ya 011-23381092.

Q: PM Kisan ke liye kaun eligible hai?
A: Jo bhi chhote ya seemaant kisaan parivaar jo kheti ke layak zameen ke malik hain, woh apply kar sakte hain. Zameen ki minimum seema nahi hai. Yojana un kisaanon ke liye hai jo zameen ke record ke mutabiq kheti layak zameen ke malik hain. Sansthapurn zameen malik, sarkari naukri karne wale, aur income tax dene wale iske liye eligible nahi hain.

Q: Main PM Kisan ke liye kaise apply kar sakta hoon?
A: Apne pas ki Common Service Centre jaye ya PM Kisan portal pmkisan.gov.in kholein. Ya phir apne zila/khetibari vibhag office ja sakte hain. Online self-registration pmkisan.gov.in/RegistrationForm.aspx par bhi ki ja sakti hai.

Q: PM Kisan registration ke liye kaunse documents chahiye?
A: Aapke pass Aadhaar card, zameen ka malikana record (khata/khasra number), bank account ka detail jisme IFSC code ho, aur Aadhaar se link mobile number chahiye. Aadhaha
