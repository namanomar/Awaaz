Q: PM Ujjwala Yojana kya hai?
A: Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) ek sarkari yojana hai jismein garib BPL mahilaon ko muft LPG gas connection diya jata hai. Isse chulhe ke dhuen se hone wali bimariyon se raksha milti hai. Nazdiki gas agency (IndianOil, HPCL, ya BPCL) mein jakar apply karein.

Q: Ujjwala Yojana ke liye kaun eligible hai?
A: BPL (Below Poverty Line) parivaron ki 18 saal se upar ki mahilayein eligible hain. Ghar mein pehle se LPG connection nahin hona chahiye. SC/ST parivar, PMAY beneficiary, Antyodaya card wale bhi apply kar sakte hain.

Q: Ujjwala Yojana ke liye kaise apply karen?
A: Nazdiki LPG gas agency mein jayen aur Ujjwala Yojana form maangen. Form bhar kar zaroori documents ke saath jama karen. Aap pmuy.gov.in par online bhi apply kar sakte hain.

Q: Ujjwala Yojana ke liye kaunse documents chahiye?
A: BPL ration card, Aadhaar card, bank khate ki passbook, aur ek passport size photo. Ek declaration bhi dena hoga ki ghar mein pehle se koi LPG connection nahin hai.

Q: Kya gas cylinder muft milta hai?
A: Connection, regulator, aur pehla cylinder muft milta hai. Uske baad refill ke liye aapko bhugtan karna hoga, lekin PAHAL scheme ke tahat subsidy seedha aapke bank khate mein aayegi.

Q: Ujjwala Yojana helpline number kya hai?
A: LPG helpline 1906 par call karein ya 18002333555 (toll-free) par sampark karein. Online shikayat ke liye pgportal.gov.in par jayen.

Q: Mera naam BPL suchi mein nahin hai. Kya mujhe connection mil sakta hai?
A: Ujjwala 2.0 mein kuch aur categories shamil ki gayi hain. Agar aap SC/ST hain, PMAY beneficiary hain, van nivaasi hain, ya pravasi mazduron ke parivar se hain, toh aap eligible ho sakte hain. Gas agency mein jakar apni paristhiti batayein.
